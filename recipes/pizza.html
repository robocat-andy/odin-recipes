Description

Pizza is a savory flatbread dish that originated in Naples, Italy, and has become one of the most popular foods worldwide. At its core, it consists of a dough base, sauce, cheese, and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt & oregano

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Let it rise
  3. Shape the base into a circle
  4. Assemble the pizza with cheese and sauce and toppings
  5. Bake
  6. Serve
