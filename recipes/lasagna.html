Description

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish that originated in italy and has become popular worldwide. It's traditionally made by stacking wide, flat pasta sheets with alternating layers of rich fillings.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
